Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the Ashtavadhani platform

How does the app compile videos locally?

Ashtavadhani packages a localized environment containing python compilers, ffmpeg, and moviepy. It executes all video render workloads directly on your computer's CPU, keeping the operations completely secure.

What are the AI Credits in the dashboard?

Google Gemini provides free credits for API requests. The app displays your remaining credits. If your credits run out, the app automatically switches to the "Free Version" and uses local pixel-motion heuristics for editing without crashing.

Is my footage uploaded to any server?

No. Unlike web-based video editors, all your raw files, clips, and audio tracks remain strictly on your local hard drive. The only network request made is a text-based semantic exchange with the Google Gemini API to calculate timeline plans.
